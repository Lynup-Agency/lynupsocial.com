Permission Marketing

Permission Marketing is the next step up from direct marketing. Now, instead of trying to force people to look at you, they’re asking you to show them something. Well, if you promise them something awesome and deliver on that promise. Lynup helps you find those people – the ones who want to pay attention to you. Then we help you deliver whatever it is they’re expecting from you – whether it’s emails that are actually informative and interesting, personalized deals, or text message updates.
